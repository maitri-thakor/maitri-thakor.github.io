Bootcamp Learning Plan

1.What is your long term goal and/or career pathway?

My long-term goal is to have a successful career in the tech industry, using the skills I learn from this course. I want to help make the tech world more diverse and contribute to cool projects. I plan to start with entry-level and mid-level jobs in software development, quality assurance, and data analytics. Ultimately, I want to become a data analyst or scientist, helping people make better decisions using data.

2.What do you think your biggest strengths and limitations will be in Bootcamp?

My biggest strength is my determination—I never give up, and I'm willing to invest time to succeed. However, I know I need to manage my time well. I'll work hard and use my time wisely to excel in Bootcamp. As for my limitations, I find it challenging to identify and resolve errors, which can be frustrating. When stuck on one topic, I struggle to focus on the next, leading to time loss in resolving errors.

3.What do you think your biggest non-technical challenge at Bootcamp will be?

My main non-technical challenge at Bootcamp will be staying focused and organized for the nine weeks. I've had interruptions during Foundations that sometimes make it hard to concentrate, but I'm hoping Bootcamp will help me get back on track. Working well in groups and speaking in front of others might also be tough for me. Even though I like teamwork, I'm worried about slowing down or asking for help while working with a team.

4.What non-technical skills - human skills - would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

I believe it's important to address weaknesses and work on areas that slow me down. Improving self-awareness is one aspect I'd like to focus on, as it can benefit various areas such as teamwork and being more comfortable asking for help, especially when working in groups. At Dev Academy, I want to work on my communication and teamwork.

5.What are your expectations from the Bootcamp team?

I expect the Bootcamp team to be as supportive and kind as the Foundations team. I hope for timely answers, helpful resources, and lots of patience, just like I've experienced so far.

6.What are your expectations of yourself on Bootcamp?

My expectations of myself on Bootcamp are to work hard, stay organized, and participate actively. I will manage my time well, stay positive, and ask for help when I need it. I want to learn and improve both technically and personally, and give my best effort every day.
