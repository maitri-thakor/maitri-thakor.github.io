Te Whare Tapa Whā

Wall Activities
Taha Tinana (Physical Wellbeing) I like to go for a walk every day at least for 45 minutes.I drink at least 6 glasses of water everyday.
Taha Whānau (Family Wellbeing) I spend quality time with my family members. This can include activities such as cooking together, watching movies, or engaging in hobbies. I visit temple on every weekend with my friends.
Taha Hinengaro (Emotional and Mental Wellbeing) Yoga is a powerful tool for promoting emotional and mental wellbeing. I always do a yoga in the morning for my self-care and self-compassion. By doing this , I feel relax and more energizes .
Taha Wairua (Spiritual Wellbeing) In the morning, I do pray to god and do some other spiritual activities according to hinduism.
Whenua (Interconnections to the land and environment) Sometimes, I like to visit different parks and beaches.
